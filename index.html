PROJECT X RULES

These are the rules that are currently put in place to ensure fairness and respect within our community.

Failure to comply with the rules will result in sanctions.

Please note that not everything is listed in here, ultimately it comes down to staff discretion.

♦-♦-♦-♦-Fail Roleplay (FRP)-♦-♦-♦-♦

FRP is the act of failing to roleplay to correct conduct or its entirety. This regards situations that would not be correct in relations to real life scenarios.

Exiting vehicles while cuffed/restrained.

Shooting or hitting someone through objects with no visibility.

Disconnecting during arrests or combat to avoid RP consequences.

♦-♦-♦-♦-Kill on Sight (KOS)-♦-♦-♦-♦

KOS (Kill On Sight) means attacking or killing another player immediately upon seeing them, without prior roleplay or warning. This is only allowed if it fits the staff-permitted scenarios; otherwise, it is prohibited and may lead to penalties

♦-♦-♦-♦-Vehicle Death Match (VDM)-♦-♦-♦-♦

VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) is using a vehicle to intentionally run over or kill other players without proper roleplay or valid in-game reasons. This behavior is prohibited and can result in penalties.

♦-♦-♦-♦-IRL Trading-♦-♦-♦-♦

IRL trading exchanging in-game items, currency, or services for real-life money or goods is strictly prohibited. Engaging in IRL trading can lead to severe penalties, including bans.

♦-♦-♦-♦-Fear Roleplay-♦-♦-♦-♦

Fear RP means your character should realistically react with fear or caution to dangerous or threatening situations. Ignoring this and acting recklessly breaks immersion and can result in penalties.

♦-♦-♦-♦-No Intention to RP (NITRP)-♦-♦-♦-♦

NITRP refers to joining the server without the intention to actively participate in roleplay. This disrupts the experience for others and is not allowed. Players found NITRP may face penalties.
